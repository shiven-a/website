News & Blog admin

How publishing works on this site: this is a plain HTML/CSS/JS website with no server, so this page can't push changes to your live site by itself. Add, edit or delete posts below, then click "Download updated blog-posts.js" — that downloads a ready-made file. Upload it to assets/js/blog-posts.js on your live site (overwriting the old one) via your host's file manager, FTP, or GitHub — that upload is the "publish" step, and the News & Blog page updates for everyone as soon as you do it.

Keep this page private: anyone who finds this URL can see your post list and use this form, though they can only download a file to their own computer — they cannot change your live site without your upload step. If you'd rather nobody stumbles onto it, ask your host how to password-protect this one page, or simply don't link to it publicly (it isn't in the main menu).
